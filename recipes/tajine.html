Tajine

Description

The tajine represents the culture and culinary tradition of Tunisia and the Maghreb. Its variations, intense flavors, and cultural importance make this dish a unique experience.

Ingredients

  • 1 tsp oil
  • 1 onion, diced
  • ½ lb chicken breast, diced (roughly one breast)
  • 1 ½ tsp coriander seed, ground
  • ¾ tsp cumin
  • ¾ tsp salt
  • ½ tsp caraway seed
  • ¼ tsp red pepper flakes
  • ¼ tsp black pepper
  • ¼ c water
  • 1 russet potato, peeled and diced
  • 8 eggs, beaten
  • 3 oz (3/4 c) Swiss cheese, grated (or other white cheese like Gruyere, Monterey Jack, Gouda, etc)
  • ½ c fresh parsley, chopped

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350⁰F.
  2. In a large skillet, heat oil. Add diced onions and sauté over medium high heat for 2-3 minutes, until they begin to soften. Add diced chicken and spices.
  3. Mix to combine.
  4. Add the water to the skillet and simmer until the liquid has evaporated, 2-3 min.
  5. Add the potatoes and sauté for an additional 2-3 minutes, until lightly tender.
  6. Remove the mixture from the heat and pour it into a greased 8x8 baking dish.
  7. Mix the beaten eggs with 2 oz (1/2 c) of grated cheese and the chopped parsley.
  8. Pour the eggs over the chicken mixture in the baking dish. Top with the remaining 1 oz of grated cheese.
  9. Bake the tajine for 35 minutes, or until the middle is set and the top is golden.
  10. Remove from the oven and let the tajine rest for 10 minutes before cutting. (This will help it set and make it easier to cut.)
  11. Cut the tajine into 8 rectangles and serve warm or cold.
